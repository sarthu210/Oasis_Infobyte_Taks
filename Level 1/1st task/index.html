Incredible India


One of the oldest civilisations in the world,
is a mosaic of multicultural experiences.
With a rich heritage and myriad attractions,
the country is among the most popular tourist
destinations in the world. It covers an area
of 32, 87,263 sq. km, extending from the
snow-covered Himalayan heights to the tropicalM
rain forests of the south. As the 7th largest
country in the world, India stands apart from
the rest of Asia, marked off as it is by mountains
and the sea, which give the country a distinct
geographical entity.

Wonders Of India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, is an Islamic ivory-white marble
mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna
in the Indian city of Agra. It was commissioned
in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house
the tomb of his favourite wife, Mumtaz Mahal;
it also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself.

India Gate

India Gate, official name Delhi Memorial,
originally called All-India War Memorial,
monumental sandstone arch in New Delhi,
dedicated to the troops of British India
who died in wars fought between 1914 and 1919.
India Gate, which is located at the eastern end
of the Rajpath (formerly called the Kingsway),
is about 138 feet (42 metres) in height.
